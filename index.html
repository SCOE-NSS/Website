About Us

National Service Scheme, Scoe Pune is a government-sponsored public service program conducted by the Ministry of Youth and Affairs and Sports of the Government of India. Our organisation aims to in still the idea of social welfare and provide service to society without bias. The volunteers of our organisation strive hard to ensure that everyone in need has an opportunity to make their pleas heard and lend a helping hand to those who do the same.
Our organisation, which comprises of enthusiastic volunteers and faculty advisors, take initiatives and strive towards a better society where development not only lies in the betterment of oneâ€™s self but in the development and the well being of the society as a whole.

OUR TEAMS

Technical Team

Provides technical support and guidance to organisation. This team supports the establishment and implementation of technical stuff in the team and initiates and provides technical guidance to the rest for the development of SCOE NSS.

Cultural Team

The Cultural Team provides a sense of identity.The Culture team shapes the way volunteers interact with the organisation through different activities.Our cultural team promotes healthy competition in different competitions conducted by different organisations.

Arts Team

The Art team gives us a creative team that expresses imaginative skill. The Art team have a diverse range of volunteer activities in creating visuals, performing artifacts, and expressing the author's imaginative mind.

Documentation Team

Our Documentation team is responsible for the writing, distribution, collection, storage, and maintenance of the documentation material of SCOE NSS.

Finance Team

A finance team manages the money in our organisation.The Finance department plans and manages unit money, making sure our unit can access money in sustainable ways.

Goods Management Team

The Goods management team are responsible for organising the storage of our unit goods.They oversee team performance, expediting the receiving and shipping of goods, and ensuring efficient, organized storage.

Social Media Team

Our Social media team personify SCOE NSS digitally. Establishes our online reputation by building posters and different stuff. Manages our social media handles.
