ABOUT US

Introduction

National Service Scheme, Scoe Pune is a government-sponsored public service program conducted by the Ministry of Youth and Affairs and Sports of the Government of India. Our organisation aims to in still the idea of social welfare and provide service to society without bias. The volunteers of our organisation strive hard to ensure that everyone in need has an opportunity to make their pleas heard and lend a helping hand to those who do the same. Our organisation, which comprises of enthusiastic volunteers and faculty advisors, take initiatives and strive towards a better society where development not only lies in the betterment of one’s self but in the development and the well being of the society as a whole. The drive to carry out such responsibilities and hold these values to the utmost level of respect, is what aligns our organisation with the moto “NOT ME BUT YOU”.
